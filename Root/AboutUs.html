Back in 1983 I started this business out of my old garage. Originally we only had one offering, "Pietro's Pepperoni Pizza", for only a dollar a slice!

From these humble beginnings, Pietro's has risen to be in over 15 locations statewide! While Pietro's Pepperoni is still a staple of our business, we now offer 15 toppings and every combination you can imagine! Come down and see us anytime and you'll see why Pietro's is the best in the business!
